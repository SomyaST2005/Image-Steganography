How does this work?
1
Huffman Encoding

Your message is compressed using variable-length codes — frequent characters get shorter codes.

2
Spread Spectrum

The binary stream is XORed with a pseudo-random sequence, spreading the signal for obfuscation.

3
LSB Embedding

The result is hidden in the least significant bits of image pixels — invisible to the eye.
